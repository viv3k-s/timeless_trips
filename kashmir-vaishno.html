Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 01

Delhi to Katra

🚕 Road/Train Transfer

Depart Delhi. Travel to Katra (470 km, ~10 hrs by train or road) — the base camp for Vaishno Devi temple. Check in to hotel. Rest and prepare for the next day's sacred trek. Overnight Katra.

Day 02

Vaishno Devi Darshan

⛰️ Sacred Trek to Bhawan

Wake up at 3 AM for the sacred 14km trek to Mata Vaishno Devi Bhawan (helicopter option available at extra cost). Receive the divine blessings of Maa Vaishno Devi. Trek back to Katra. Rest and recover. Overnight Katra.

Day 03

Katra to Srinagar

✈️ Valley Bound

Drive from Katra to Srinagar (215 km, ~5.5 hrs) via the scenic Jammu-Srinagar National Highway, crossing the famous Banihal Tunnel. Arrive and check in to your houseboat on Dal Lake. Evening shikara ride. Overnight houseboat.

Day 04

Srinagar Sightseeing

🎈 Mughal Gardens

Visit Shalimar Bagh, Nishat Bagh, Chashme Shahi and Hazratbal Shrine. Afternoon Shankaracharya Temple. Evening at Dal Lake market for saffron and local crafts. Overnight houseboat.

Day 05

Srinagar to Pahalgam

🏞 Betaab Valley

Drive to Pahalgam. Visit Betaab Valley, Aru Valley and Chandanwari. Evening walk by Lidder River. Overnight Pahalgam.

Day 06

Pahalgam to Gulmarg

⛽ Snow Meadows

Drive to Gulmarg. Gondola ride (Phases 1 & 2) for snow and mountain views. Snow activities. Overnight Gulmarg.

Day 07

Gulmarg Leisure

🏕️ Last Day in the Mountains

Morning snow play, horse riding on meadows. Afternoon drive to Tangmarg. Last evening souvenir shopping for Kashmiri crafts. Overnight Gulmarg.

Day 08

Return to Delhi

✈️ Departure

Drive to Srinagar airport or train station for your return journey. Tour ends with the blessings of Mata Vaishno Devi and memories of paradise on earth!

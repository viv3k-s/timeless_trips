Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 01

Bangkok Arrival → Pattaya

✈️ Arrival · 🚕 Transfer to Pattaya

Arrive at Suvarnabhumi Airport, Bangkok, where our representative will warmly welcome you. Board your private vehicle for the approximately 2-hour scenic drive to Pattaya along the Eastern Seaboard. Check in to the magnificent Centara Grand Mirage Beach Resort — a spectacular 5-star beachfront property inspired by a pirate-era adventure theme. Spend the rest of the day settling in, enjoy the resort's multiple pools, and explore Walking Street in the evening. Overnight at hotel.

Day 02

Coral Island Tour by Speedboat

🏝 Speedboat · 🏖️ Snorkelling · 🍽️ Indian Lunch (Optional)

After breakfast, embark on a full-day Coral Island (Koh Larn) Tour by speedboat. Enjoy crystal-clear turquoise waters, white sandy beaches and vibrant coral reefs. Activities available at the island include snorkelling, parasailing, banana boat rides, jet-skiing and sea-walking (payable directly). Enjoy a delicious Indian lunch on the island (optional, extra cost). Return to Pattaya in the late afternoon. Evening free to explore the beachfront promenade. Overnight at hotel.

Day 03

Pattaya at Leisure

🗺 Free Day · Optional: Alcazar Cabaret Show

After breakfast, enjoy a completely free day to explore Pattaya at your own pace. Visit the famous Sanctuary of Truth (wooden temple masterpiece), Nong Nooch Tropical Garden, Big Buddha Hill, or simply relax on the beach and enjoy the resort's world-class amenities. In the evening, the Alcazar Cabaret Show is highly recommended — a spectacular performance featuring elaborate costumes, dance and live music (optional, payable directly). Overnight at hotel.

Day 04

Pattaya → Bangkok → Departure

🚕 Transfer · ✈️ Onward Flight

After breakfast, check out from the hotel and transfer to Suvarnabhumi Airport, Bangkok, for your onward flight back to Delhi. Our representative will assist you at the airport. Tour ends with memories unlimited!

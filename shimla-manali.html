Trip Itinerary

Day 01

Delhi to Shimla

En Route Sightseeing

Upon arrival at Delhi Airport/Railway Station, you will be picked up and driven to Shimla by road (~9 hours). En route, enjoy brief sightseeing stops including a visit to a famous waterfall. Upon reaching Shimla, check in at your hotel and relax. In the evening, spend some leisure time exploring Mall Road to experience the vibrant charm of Shimla. Afterward, return to the hotel for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 02

Shimla & Kufri

Full Day Sightseeing

After breakfast, head to Kufri (approx. 2,500 m, ~1 hour from Shimla) and visit the Himalayan Nature Park to admire wildlife and the scenic Green Valley. Return to Shimla in the afternoon and explore the historic Vice Regal Lodge (now Rashtrapati Niwas) with its colonial-era artefacts. Then stroll along Mall Road and visit Christ Church, Jakhu Temple, Lakkar Bazaar, Scandal Point, Gaiety Theatre and Town Hall. Evening at leisure. Return for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 03

Shimla to Manali via Kullu

En Route Sightseeing

After breakfast, begin your scenic journey to Manali (~8 hours). En route, explore the charming town of Kullu — "Valley of Gods" — perched at about 1,230 m and formed by the Beas River. On the way, drive past the Beas-Sutlej Link Project, Pandoh Dam and Sundernagar Lake. Upon arrival in Manali, check in at your hotel and relax. Overnight stay.

Day 04

Solang Valley Excursion

Snow Point & Adventure

After breakfast, proceed to explore the breathtaking Solang Valley (Snow Point), along with visits to Nehru Kund and Jogini Waterfall. Spend the day enjoying snow-covered landscapes. Here you can indulge in snow scootering, paragliding, zorbing, skiing and more (on direct payment basis). Return to Manali for overnight stay.

Day 05

Manali Local Sightseeing

Temples, Monasteries & Mall Road

After breakfast, embark on a full-day tour visiting the 450-year-old Hadimba Devi Temple, Vaishisht Temple, Van Vihar, Tibetan Monasteries, Mall Road, Club House, Old Manali and Manu Temple. Explore the monastery and local handicraft centre before strolling through scenic Vashisht village. Return to hotel for overnight stay.

Day 06

Manali to Delhi

Departure

After breakfast at your hotel, check out and begin the journey back to Delhi. You will be dropped at the airport or railway station for your onward travel. We hope this journey created memories that will last a lifetime!

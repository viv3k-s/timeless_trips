Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 01

Dubai Arrival

🚕 Private Airport Transfer

Board your flight from your respective hub and arrive in Dubai. Our representative will meet you at arrivals and transfer you to your hotel in Bur Dubai by private cab. Check in, freshen up, and take the evening at leisure to soak in the dazzling Dubai skyline. Overnight at hotel.

Day 02

Museum of the Future & Premium Desert Safari

🏜 Museum · 🏕️ Desert Safari · 🌙 BBQ Dinner

After breakfast, head out for sightseeing at the Museum of the Future — marvel at its iconic ring-shaped stainless-steel architecture. Step inside and experience the visionary ambiance of OSS Hope on the 5th floor via a simulated space shuttle, where you'll witness captivating missions, discoveries and inventions of humanity's future.

In the evening, proceed for the Premium Desert Safari (VIP seating) featuring sand-dune bashing, Taruna cultural show, belly dance performances and a lavish BBQ dinner under the desert stars. Return to hotel for overnight stay.

Day 03

Marina Dhow Cruise with Dinner

⛵️ Luxury Cruise · 🍽️ Buffet Dinner · 🎶 Live Dance

After breakfast, enjoy a free morning to explore Bur Dubai's historic markets, the Gold Souq or simply relax at the hotel. In the evening, board a luxury Lotus Marina Yacht for an enchanting dhow cruise along Dubai Marina — complete with a lavish buffet dinner and a spectacular live dance performance. Return to hotel for overnight stay.

Day 04

Day at Leisure

🗺 Explore Dubai at Your Own Pace

A completely free day to explore Dubai on your own. Optional activities (at extra cost) include: Dubai Mall & Burj Khalifa 124th floor observation deck, Dubai Frame, Palm Jumeirah Monorail, Atlantis Aquaventure Waterpark, Global Village or a private city tour. Our team can assist with arrangements. Overnight at hotel.

Day 05

Day at Leisure

🗺 Optional: Shopping, Spa, Desert Ride

Another free day to indulge — visit the iconic Dubai Mall, explore the traditional Spice Souq, enjoy a spa afternoon, or simply relax poolside at the hotel. Overnight at hotel.

Day 06

Dubai Departure

✈️ Airport Transfer · Tour End

After breakfast, check out from your hotel and transfer to Dubai International Airport by private cab for your onward flight. Tour ends with unlimited memories of the City of Superlatives!

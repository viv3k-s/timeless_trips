Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 01

Delhi to Manali

🚕 Overnight Journey

Depart Delhi by comfortable Volvo bus or private cab in the evening. The scenic overnight journey takes you through Chandigarh and Mandi. Enjoy the mountains unfolding as you arrive. Overnight travel.

Day 02

Arrival in Manali & Rest

🏛 Check-in & Leisure

Arrive Manali in the morning. Check in to your cosy couple-friendly hotel. After freshening up, enjoy a romantic stroll along the Beas river. In the evening, explore Mall Road — perfect for shopping, street food and sunset views. Candlelit dinner at a riverside restaurant. Overnight at hotel.

Day 03

Solang Valley & Snow Activities

🏕️ Adventure & Romance

Post breakfast, head to Solang Valley (Snow Point) — a magical winter landscape. Enjoy snow scootering, zorbing, horse riding and cable car rides (payable directly). Pack a picnic and watch the sunset over the Himalayas. Return to hotel for a romantic dinner. Overnight.

Day 04

Rohtang Pass Excursion

🏔 High Altitude Adventure

Subject to permit and weather, visit the iconic Rohtang Pass (3,978m) for breathtaking snow views (permit charges extra). En route stop at Rahala Falls. Alternatively visit Naggar Castle and Kullu Valley. Return for a relaxing evening at the hotel.

Day 05

Manali Sightseeing

🏛 Temples & Old Town

After breakfast, visit the ancient 450-year-old Hadimba Devi Temple set in a cedar forest, the serene Manu Temple, Vashisht hot springs and the Tibetan Monastery. Stroll through quaint Old Manali lanes, browse handicrafts and savour local Himachali cuisine.

Day 06

Manali to Delhi Departure

✈️ Fond Farewell

After breakfast, check out and begin your return journey to Delhi. Tour ends with memories of your romantic Himalayan escape that will last a lifetime!
